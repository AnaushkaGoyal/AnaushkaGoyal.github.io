Spatial Lag to 2011 Development

This map measures how close each fishnet cell is to 2011 development by computing a spatial lag distance from cell centroids. Values are symbolized using quintile breaks to highlight areas with stronger proximity effects versus areas that are more disconnected from existing development patterns.
