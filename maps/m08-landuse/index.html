Land Use & Bikeshare Stations

This map examines how bikeshare station distribution aligns with underlying land use patterns. By layering station locations over a categorized land use base, the analysis reveals where infrastructure supports dense, mixed-use neighborhoods and where spatial gaps remain in residential or peripheral zones.
