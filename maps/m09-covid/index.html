COVID Center Accessibility

This analysis identifies candidate locations for new COVID service centers using network accessibility and proximity to population. Proposed sites are evaluated through service-area coverage so that residents are within ~30 minutes of a center, helping reduce gaps in access across neighborhoods with high concentrations of people and key trip generators.
